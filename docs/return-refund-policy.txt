AMPLIFY TOTAL EXPERIENCE
Return & Refund Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026
This policy explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds work for purchases
made from Amplify Total Experience. It applies to all orders unless a product
page or promotion states otherwise.


1. RETURN WINDOW
You may return most items within 30 calendar days of the delivery date for a
refund or exchange. The 30-day window begins on the date the carrier marks the
order as delivered. Returns requested after 30 days may be offered store credit
at our discretion, but are not guaranteed.


2. CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
To qualify for a full refund, items must be returned:
  - in their original condition and original packaging,
  - with all accessories, manuals, and free gifts included, and
  - unused or only lightly inspected.

Opened items ARE eligible for return within the 30-day window as long as the
item is undamaged and complete. Items that are used beyond reasonable
inspection, damaged by the customer, or missing parts may receive a partial
refund or be declined.


3. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following are final sale and cannot be returned or refunded:
  - gift cards and digital downloads,
  - personalized or custom-configured products,
  - items marked "Final Sale" or "Clearance" at the time of purchase, and
  - hygiene-sensitive items (e.g., earbuds/headphones) once the seal is broken.


4. HOW TO START A RETURN
1) Have your order number ready (for example, ORD-1001).
2) Contact us by phone, chat, or through the self-service assistant.
3) We will email a prepaid return label and return instructions.
4) Pack the item securely and drop it off with the carrier within 7 days of
   receiving the label.


5. REFUND METHOD AND TIMING
Once we receive and inspect your return, we process approved refunds within
3-5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. After we
issue the refund, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for your bank or
card issuer to post the credit to your account.

If you paid with a gift card or store credit, the refund is returned as store
credit.


6. RETURN SHIPPING COSTS
Return shipping is FREE when the return is due to our error (wrong, defective,
or damaged item). For change-of-mind or "no longer needed" returns, a flat
return shipping fee of $6.99 is deducted from the refund. Original shipping
charges are non-refundable except when the return is due to our error.


7. RESTOCKING FEE
Most returns have no restocking fee. A restocking fee of up to 15% may apply to
opened large electronics and to items returned without their original packaging.


8. EXCHANGES
We are happy to exchange an item for a different size, color, or model within
the 30-day window, subject to availability. If the new item costs more, you pay
the difference; if it costs less, we refund the difference to your original
payment method.


9. DAMAGED, DEFECTIVE, OR INCORRECT ITEMS
If your item arrives damaged, defective, or is not what you ordered, contact us
within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or a full refund,
including original shipping, at no cost to you. Photos of the damage help us
resolve the issue faster.


10. LATE OR MISSING REFUNDS
If you have not received a refund you expected:
  - check your bank account or card statement again,
  - contact your bank or card issuer, as posting times vary, and
  - if you have done both and still have not received it, contact us and we
    will trace the refund.


11. ORDER STATUS AND REFUND ELIGIBILITY
Orders that are already marked delivered may be returned within the 30-day
window. Orders still in "processing" or "shipped" status can often be canceled
for a full refund before they are delivered - contact us as soon as possible.
Some items are marked non-refundable at the time of sale; those are not eligible
for a refund but may still be exchanged if defective.
